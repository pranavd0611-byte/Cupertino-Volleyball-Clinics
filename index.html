Intro to Positions

Understanding the different roles on the court.

Setter

The "quarterback" of the team. Runs the offense by setting the ball for attackers.

Outside Hitter

Attacks from the left side. Needs strong passing/defensive skills and powerful hitting.

Middle Blocker

The first line of defense. Focuses on blocking at the net and running quick tempo attacks.

Opposite Hitter

Attacks from the right side. Balances the offense and helps block the opposing outside hitter.

Libero

Defensive and passing specialist. Wears a different colored jersey and plays mostly in the back row.
